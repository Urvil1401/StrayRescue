OUR GOAL

Forging new lives for animals

Stray Rescue is a website through which we are trying to provide help to the stray animals. We help in animal rescue, neutering, adoption, reporting animal abusers and encouraging people to treat animals nicely. We are heading a step towards the betterment of the stray animals which are helpless and need care and support.
